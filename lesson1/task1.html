Today I would like to share some information about bullfinch specifically.

Reasons to love bullfinches:

  • they are easy to tame and are not afraid of humans
  • live in areas with trees only
  • fast eaters - can eat up to 30 flowers buds in a minute
  • 3 weeks old chicks are ready to live separately already
  • tiny birds - their weight reaches up to 30 g
